Goals and Objectives

Cultivate Innovation

The IEDC Hackathon aims to foster a culture of creativity and problem-solving, inspiring participants to think outside the box.

Build Connections

The event provides a platform for participants to network, collaborate, and form lasting partnerships with like-minded individuals.

Empower Next Gen

By nurturing the skills and ideas of young innovators, the hackathon contributes to the development of future tech leaders.
